What we’re building

Brinkmann Bank is an early-stage initiative exploring a new kind of European-first banking experience for founders.

We focus on one thing: helping serious businesses move fast, without losing control, compliance or long-term perspective.

The initial scope

  • Business banking (via BaaS)
    Business accounts, payments and cards — built on regulated Banking-as-a-Service partners.
  • Governance by design
    Clear structures, audit-readiness and decision traceability embedded from day one.
  • Founder support
    Calm, informed guidance on financial and strategic decisions — not hype, not shortcuts.

What this is (and isn’t)

Brinkmann Bank is not a consumer bank, not a neobank clone, and not a growth-at-all-costs experiment.

This is a long-term project, built carefully, in phases.
