We’re building

The MVP is deliberately focused. Three things, done properly — so it actually feels trustworthy.

1
Business banking that doesn’t waste your week. Accounts, payments and cashflow visibility — powered by a regulated BaaS partner.
2
Governance by design (not a bolt-on checkbox). Decision trails, role clarity, and compliance awareness from the start.
3
A founder assistant that improves decisions — not vibes. Practical prompts for cashflow, risk and operations. Less “AI magic”, more “useful”.
