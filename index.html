Why Brinkmann Bank exists

Starting a business is hard enough. Banking, compliance and governance shouldn’t slow founders down — yet they often do.

Founders are expected to make structural decisions early, while the consequences show up much later: audits, funding, regulatory requirements, and operational complexity.

Brinkmann Bank exists to make those early decisions clear, structured and defensible.

Important: Brinkmann Bank is not a licensed bank. We are an independent initiative focused on banking structure, governance readiness and compliance awareness.

What we are building

Banking structure

A clear setup for business accounts, payments and roles — designed to scale with your company, not against it.

Governance readiness

Decision logging, policy basics, and an audit-friendly trail from day one — without turning founders into compliance officers.

Founder guidance

Practical support for operational and governance choices founders must make early — when advice is scarce and mistakes get expensive.

Where relevant, services may rely on regulated partners (e.g., Banking-as-a-Service) for licensed components. Details depend on jurisdiction and product scope.

Who it’s for

Built for serious starters and early-stage founders who want to set the foundation properly: clear ownership, clear responsibilities, and fewer surprises later.
