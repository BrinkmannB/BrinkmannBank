Why Brinkmann Bank exists

Starting a company is hard enough. Banking, compliance and governance shouldn’t slow founders down — yet they often do.

Founders are expected to make structural decisions early, while the consequences only surface later: audits, funding rounds, regulatory scrutiny or operational complexity.

Brinkmann Bank exists to make those early decisions clear, deliberate and defensible.

Important: Brinkmann Bank is an independent initiative and not a licensed bank. Where licensed services are involved, these are provided by regulated partners.

What we are building

Banking structure

Clear setup for business accounts, roles and payments — designed to scale with your company, not against it.

Governance readiness

Decision logging, policy basics and an audit-friendly trail from day one — without turning founders into compliance officers.

Founder guidance

Practical support for the financial and governance choices founders must make early — when advice is scarce and mistakes get expensive.

Pre-launch scope: clarity, structure and readiness. Product and partner details depend on jurisdiction and rollout.

Who it’s for

Built for serious starters and early-stage founders who want to set the foundation properly: clear ownership, clear responsibilities and fewer surprises later.
