Why Brinkmann Bank exists

Starting a company today means dealing with banks that weren’t built for founders. You open an account, and you’re left alone with compliance and the consequences of AI-driven decisions.

Founders are expected to move fast — while the system is designed to slow them down. That’s broken.

Brinkmann Bank exists because modern businesses need more than an IBAN: embedded governance, clear accountability, and infrastructure you can actually build on.

What we’re building

Business banking that actually fits founders

Accounts, SEPA payments and cards — powered by a regulated Banking-as-a-Service partner. Clean setup, clear roles, and no “enterprise theatre”.

Governance built in, not bolted on

Decision trails, access control and audit-ready evidence by default. So compliance becomes a capability — not a last-minute panic.

Founder support that doesn’t guess

Practical guidance around cash, risk and operations — with accountability. Not “AI magic”, but tools that help you make defensible decisions.

Who it’s for

Brinkmann Bank is for founders, freelancers and early-stage teams building real businesses — the ones who want speed and standards.
